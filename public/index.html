About 9143

Hi! I'm the creator behind 9143. To me, this name is more than just a string of numbers it represents joy and my love for life. "91" sounds like the word "Joy," and "43" is my lucky number.

I love using knitting, crocheting, and even various materials to create vibrant and colorful pieces. For me, it's not just a craft; it's my way of expressing myself and exploring endless possibilities.

At 9143, I'm not just making products.I want to share my passion for life with every stitch. If you enjoy creative, colorful, and unconventional styles, I hope my work can be a part of your life, bringing you little bits of joy and surprise.
